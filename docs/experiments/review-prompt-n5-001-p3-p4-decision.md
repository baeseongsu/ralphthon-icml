## P0–P2 trajectory

- P0 established the frozen baseline.
- P1 added shared evidence-to-ordinal calibration. Although all aggregate
  components improved, significance agreement regressed by `-0.066667`, beyond
  the `0.05` dimension gate, so P1 was discarded.
- P2 retained the useful evidence calibration while separating significance
  from soundness and originality and requiring direct positive impact evidence.
  Relative to P0, Composite improved by `+0.051444`, reference-score agreement by
  `+0.064000`, and Judge review quality by `+0.038889`, with no gate regression.
  P2 therefore became—and remained—the kept parent.

## P3 decision

P3 was generated by the first memory-driven reflection pass. It rewrote all
seven `Review sections` around explicit claim-evidence-decision chains, precise
artifact locators, severity/scope language, and separation of author claims from
demonstrated results.

Relative to P2:

- Composite: `+0.002000`
- Reference-score agreement: `-0.029333`
- Judge review quality: `+0.033333`
- Soundness agreement: `-0.066667`
- Overall-recommendation agreement: `-0.080000`
- Score-rationale-consistency Judge mean: `4.4 → 5.0`

The prose-quality hypothesis was supported, but the generated scores became too
conservative. Human agreement regressed beyond the `0.02` component gate, while
soundness and overall recommendation also exceeded their dimension regression
limits. P3 therefore remains an experimental prompt and cannot become the parent.

## P4 decision

P4 reflected on the P3 failure and attempted a lighter-weight intervention. It
limited claim-evidence requirements to central or score-driving observations,
kept `summary` descriptive, and retained the optional-evidence guardrail.

Relative to P2:

- Composite: `-0.007444`
- Reference-score agreement: `-0.042667`
- Judge review quality: `+0.027778`
- Soundness agreement: `-0.133333`
- Overall-recommendation agreement: `-0.080000`

P4 preserved much of the Judge-quality gain but further reduced soundness
agreement and did not improve Composite. It was discarded by the primary
objective before any regression exception could be considered.

## Meta-level experience

- Requiring precise claim/evidence/locator links across every review section can
  improve specificity, question quality, limitations coverage, and rationale
  consistency.
- On this small fixed sample, the same rules shift ordinal judgments toward
  lower soundness and overall-recommendation scores.
- Merely describing the intervention as lighter weight was insufficient; the
  operational burden still caused conservative score behavior.
- A future campaign should decouple prose-structure requirements from score
  severity, or add an explicit calibration rule stating that evidence-trace
  detail and uncertainty language must not themselves lower a score.
- These are N=5 diagnostic observations, not general performance claims.
